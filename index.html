About Us

Mar Coco is a small brand focused on creating practical and stylish products for swimmers and water lovers. Our goal is to combine comfort, durability, and simple design so that every product can be used both for training and for enjoying time at the pool or the beach. We specialize in handmade swimming gear such as swim leggings, bags for carrying swimming equipment, swim caps, and other useful accessories. Each product is carefully designed with attention to quality and functionality, making sure it meets the needs of people who enjoy being in the water.

At Mar Coco, we believe that swimming is more than just a sport — it is a lifestyle. That is why we aim to create products that help swimmers feel comfortable, confident, and ready for every swim.
